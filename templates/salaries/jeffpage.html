About

The World-Herald has requested calendar-year 2013 payroll data from several public agencies in Nebraska and Iowa. Citizens can use this website to inspect the salaries of employees at these agencies as they are released. The figures are current as of Dec. 31, 2013.

Names have been withheld for employees making less than $50,000 a year — the median household income in Nebraska, roughly — to highlight employees with the biggest impact on payroll budgets.

Pay components are divided into base salary and other compensation, which if not otherwise specified can include things like overtime, bonuses, longevity pay and uniform allowances.
